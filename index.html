Behind the lens

The person behind the camera

Scientist by training.
Artist by nature.

Hi, I'm Dayna — and yes, my day job involves cells and a microscope. But both science and photography have always had my heart, and honestly? They're not that different. Both are about looking closely at something and finding what's really there.

My love for photography has always been personal. It's about freezing a moment in time — and the beautiful thing is, everyone who sees that photo takes something different from it. Same image, a thousand perspectives. That's what hooked me.

I love art. I love creating. I love sharing in moments worth capturing.

— Dayna S. McKenzie
