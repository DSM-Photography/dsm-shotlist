Terms & Conditions

1. Contract Delivery and Booking Confirmation

DSM Photography will deliver this agreement within 48 hours of booking confirmation. A 50% non-refundable deposit is due within 48 hours of the client signing this agreement. The booking is not confirmed and the date is not held until both the signed contract and deposit have been received. No verbal agreements constitute a booking.

2. Deposit and Payment

A non-refundable deposit of 50% of the total contracted balance secures the booking date. The remaining balance is due no later than 7 days prior to the session date. If the remaining balance is not received within 7 days of the session date, DSM Photography will issue a written notice. If payment is not received within 24 hours of that notice, DSM Photography reserves the right to cancel the booking.

3. Cancellation Policy

The deposit is non-refundable under all circumstances. If the client cancels 14 or more days prior to the session date, no further balance is owed. If the client cancels within 14 days of the session, the full contract balance becomes due immediately. The client is entitled to one reschedule opportunity provided written notice is given at least 14 days prior to the original session date.

4. Photographer Cancellation

In the unlikely event that DSM Photography must cancel due to emergency, illness, or circumstances beyond reasonable control, the client will receive a full refund of all payments made.

5. Image Delivery and Feedback Window

All images are delivered via private PASS gallery. The client will confirm receipt of the gallery link in writing within 48 hours of delivery notification. The client has 14 days from gallery delivery to raise any concerns. Raw or unedited files are not available for release under any circumstances.

6. Copyright, Image Usage, and Licensing

DSM Photography retains full copyright of all images captured. The client receives a limited personal license for personal, non-commercial use. Organizational use is permitted with photo credit. A commercial use license is required only for paid advertising or revenue-generating campaigns.

7. Social Media Credit

When sharing images on social media, the client agrees to credit DSM Photography by caption or tag. Preferred credit: Photo by DSM Photography / @DSM_photography.

8. Travel Fees

Travel within 30 miles of Miami, FL is included. Sessions 30–60 miles from Miami incur a $0.75/mile round trip fee. Sessions beyond 60 miles are quoted individually.

9–17. Additional Terms

Full liability, force majeure, model release, pre-event communication, testimonials, amendment, dispute resolution (Miami-Dade County, FL), governing law (State of Florida), and entire agreement clauses apply as stated in the full agreement.

11. Model Release

By signing, the client grants DSM Photography permission to use select delivered images for portfolio, website, and social media promotional purposes. Clients who wish to opt out must notify DSM Photography in writing prior to the session date.
